TTIC Student Workshop

February 8, 2019

Update

Congratulations to the award winners!

Best Talk Award: Pedro Savarese and Omar Montasser
Best Poster Award: Shubham Toshniwal
The 3rd TTIC Student workshop will take place on February 8, 2019 on the 5th floor at TTIC. Talks and panel will be in room 526. Posters and refreshments will be in the 5th floor common area. There are a total of 9 talks and 4 poster presentations. Talks will be 25 minutes long: 20 min talk, 4 min questions, 1 min turnover. There will be awards for the best poster and best talk.

Organizing Committee: Blake Woodworth, Shubham Toshniwal, Karen Livescu, Madhur Tulsiani, Erica Cocom
Talk Award Committee: Sam Wiseman, Steve Hanneke, Mesrob Ohannessian
Poster Award Committee: Michael Yu, Arturs Backurs
